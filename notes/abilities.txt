Activation Type
- Instant: The effect happens immediately
- Toggled: The effect happens while the toggle is enabled
    - Effect happens immediately
    - Has a max duration for which it can be enabled
- Channeled/Prepared
    - Channel for n turns
    - Effect happens after the channel duration
    - Can be interrupted
